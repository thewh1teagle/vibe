# Vibe

Official website: https://thewh1teagle.github.io/vibe/
Project repository: https://github.com/thewh1teagle/vibe

Vibe is a local-first transcription app for audio and video.

Key pages:
- Home: https://thewh1teagle.github.io/vibe/
- Features: https://thewh1teagle.github.io/vibe/features
- Documentation: https://thewh1teagle.github.io/vibe/docs
- Privacy Policy: https://thewh1teagle.github.io/vibe/privacy_policy.pdf

Key capabilities:
- Audio and video transcription
- URL transcription
- Speaker diarization
- AI assistant summaries
- Multiple export formats
- Multilingual support
- Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux
